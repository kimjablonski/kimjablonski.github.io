insignificant valuables

‘insignificant valuables’ is a small collection of photos that I am most proud of and that represent my photography interest. My photography can be described as, capturing the small and simple moments, finding the in the small and minute details or imperfections. I enjoy taking up close focused photos with simple subject matter where the beauty lies in the often-overlooked details. Capturing a single simple moment of beauty.
